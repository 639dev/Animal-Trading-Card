Ants

Ants have superhuman strength. They have the ability to carry between 10 and 50 times their own body weight!

  • Sientific Name: Formicidae
  • Average Length: 0.75 to 52 millimetres
  • Average Lifespan: Depends on the caste
  • Habitat: Throughout the world

Ants are common insects, but they have some unique capabilities. More than 10,000 known ant species occur around the world. They are especially prevalent in tropical forests, where they may be up to half of all the insects living in some locations. Ants inspire me enormously; they are so small but can do so much.
